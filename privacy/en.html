AnewSpan Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

AnewSpan is designed for on-device health tracking. This privacy policy applies to App Store review and the public hosted copy.

Data AnewSpan Reads

With your permission, AnewSpan reads selected Apple Health data such as weight, sleep, exercise minutes, workouts, active energy, and heart rate. The app uses this data to display weight, sleep, exercise, event, and goal trends.

AnewSpan requests read-only HealthKit access. It does not write data to Apple Health.

On-Device Processing

HealthKit data, cached summaries, and analysis results are processed on your device. Avatar images are stored locally by default. Exercise and Sleep widgets read only the minimal derived snapshots shared by the app through the local App Group.

AnewSpan does not upload your HealthKit data to an AnewSpan server. AnewSpan does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or remote logging.

This release does not provide iCloud Sync. Your goals, profile details, avatar image, unit preferences, event records, and custom event tag presets remain on your device.

Local Storage and Deletion

AnewSpan stores app preferences, profile details, goals, events, cached summaries, and avatar images locally on your device. You can edit or delete available local records in the app, restore the default avatar from profile settings, or delete the app to remove its local app data from the device.

Apple Health data remains managed by Apple Health. You can revoke AnewSpan’s HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices.
