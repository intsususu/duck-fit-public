DuckFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

DuckFit is designed for on-device health tracking. This privacy policy applies to App Store review and the public hosted copy.

Data DuckFit Reads

With your permission, DuckFit reads selected Apple Health data such as weight, sleep, exercise minutes, workouts, active energy, and heart rate. The app uses this data to display weight, sleep, exercise, event, goal, and habit trends.

DuckFit requests read-only HealthKit access. It does not write data to Apple Health.

The DuckFit widget may read today’s exercise minutes so it can automatically complete the Exercise check-in when your local threshold is met.

On-Device Processing

Health data, local events, profile details, goals, unit preferences, avatar images, analysis results, and self-discipline check-in records are processed on your device. Widget check-ins are stored in the local App Group shared between the app and widget.

DuckFit does not upload your health data or local records to a server. DuckFit does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or remote logging.

Local Storage and Deletion

DuckFit stores app preferences, profile details, goals, events, cached summaries, avatar images, and check-in records locally on your device. You can edit or delete available local records in the app, restore the default avatar from profile settings, or delete the app to remove its local app data from the device.

Apple Health data remains managed by Apple Health. You can revoke DuckFit’s HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices.
